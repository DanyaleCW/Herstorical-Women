Herstorical Women

There have been so many women who have contributed to history around the world. Many of those women have rarely been heard of or their stories have been marginalized or even worse, attributed to men. My goal is to learn about as many of these women as possible and to educate as many people as I can. This site is a passion project and I am looking forward to all the knowledge and insite I am going to gain. Thanks for coming along for the journey.
