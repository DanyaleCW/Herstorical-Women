Herstorical Women

There have been so many women who have contributed to "history" around the world. Many of those women have rarely been heard of or their stories have been marginalized or even worse, attributed to men. My goal was to learn about as many of the women as posible and to educate as many other people as I could. This site is a passion project and I am looking forward to all the knowledge and insite I am going to gain. Thanks for coming along for the journey.
